COMP 4441 Computer Music

Lab 5: Vibrato and Frequency Modulation

What to do?

In this lab, you will implement an FM trumpet and clarinet with vibrato.

Lab Assignment - FM Clarinet

  • Make an FM clarinet based on John Chowning's FM design.
  • Refer to P. 4 of lecture notes "FM Synthesis2", obtain the parameters for a FM clarinet.
  • The sco file is given to you. It plays an octave from C4 to C5.
  • Fix the modulation index to 4.
  • Refer to P.8 of lecture notes "FM Synthesis1", add vibrato to the clarinet.
  • Make the p8 be the vibrato rate in Hz.
  • Make the p9 be the vibrato width in terms of the percentage of the fundamental frequency.
  • Starting with this template, implements the orc file and save it as fm_clarinet.orc.
  • The resulting sound should sound like this.
