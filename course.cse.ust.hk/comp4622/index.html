Course Information

    Course Descriptions

    Multicast and broadcast algorithms; Peer-to-Peer Networking; Wireless and Mobile Communication; Multimedia requirements; Advanced Internet Protocols (Diffserv, IntServ, etc.); Introduction to Network Security and Wireless Security; Sensor and Sensor Networks. Exclusion: COMP5611;

    Prerequisites

    COMP4621 or ELEC4120

    Textbook
      Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach (5th Edition)
    James F. Kurose and Keith W. Ross
    Pearson
    ISBN: 0-13-607967-9
